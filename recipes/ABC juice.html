Grilled Cheese Sandwich


Description

A cheese sandwich is a sandwich made with cheese between slices of bread. Typically, semi-hard cheeses are used for the filling, such as Cheddar, Red Leicester, or Double Gloucester. A Guardian article described the cheese sandwich as a "British lunchtime staple."


Ingredients

  • Good bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on the outside of the bread
  2. Add grated cheese
  3. Choose the right skillet
  4. Go low and slow
  5. Cover the pan
  6. Adjust the heat as needed
