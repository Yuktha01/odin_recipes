ABC Juice


Description

ABC Juice is a simple yet delicious juice that has three main ingredients, Apple, Beet, and Carrot with a hint of lemon and ginger.


Ingredients

  • 1 small or 1/2 large Apple (red or green)
  • 1/2 small Beet, peeled and chopped
  • 1 large or 2 small Carrots, chopped
  • 1/2 small Lemon or Lime (approx. 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice)
  • 1 inch piece of Ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Water

Steps

  1. Cut apple, beetroot, and carrot into big pieces.
  2. Place 1 chopped apple, 1/2 chopped beetroot, and 1 large chopped carrot in the big jar of a blender or a mixer grinder. Add 1 tablespoon lemon juice (juice from 1/2 small lime or lemon), 1 inch piece chopped ginger, and 1/2 cup water.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Take a large bowl and keep a fine metal strainer over it. Spread a cheesecloth over it and pour the prepared mixture.
  5. Gather the cloth from all the sides towards the center and twist and press it to squeeze out the juice. You can strain the juice using a nut milk bag as well.
  6. Discard the fibrous pulp. You can use this pulp to make paratha dough or add to soup as well.
  7. Pour the juice into serving glasses and serve immediately to get the maximum benefits. You can store this juice in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
